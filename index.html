How the Model Works

Every data point is scored on 5 dimensions: carrier match, exact airport, destination region, weight slab, and recency. Source type adds a reliability weight — confirmed quotes score highest (1.0×), rate sheet uploads lower (0.5×).

Override bias: when procurement edits the AI suggestion, the delta is recorded per lane. Future suggestions on that lane are adjusted by the average correction signal.
